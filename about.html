About Travel NZ

Creating unforgettable journeys across New Zealand with trusted local expertise and exceptional customer service.

Our Story

Travel NZ was established with a simple goal: to help visitors discover the incredible beauty, culture, and adventure that New Zealand has to offer. From breathtaking mountains and crystal-clear lakes to vibrant cities and rich Māori heritage, we create memorable experiences for travellers from around the world.

Over the years, we have welcomed thousands of happy travellers by providing carefully planned itineraries, knowledgeable local guides, comfortable accommodation, and outstanding customer service.

Every tour is designed to give visitors the opportunity to experience New Zealand safely, comfortably, and with unforgettable memories that last a lifetime.
